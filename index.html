Nicco Mantaño

Fighting for her people

Nicco Mantaño in her documentary, Warrior Spirit
Nicco Mantaño is a UFC champion of Navajo, Chicksaw, and Hispanic decent. She was born on December 16th 1988 in Arizona. She began her MMA journey at Durango Mixed Martail arts academy. Mantaño started with boxing and eventually took up brazilian jujistu. In 2017 she fought Roxanne Modafferi and won by unanimous decision which made her the champion of her weight class. Following her victory she decided to open a utility company with the hope to bring water, electricity, and wifi to the Navajo Nation. Following her successes Albuquerque filmaker Landon Dyksterhouse created a documentary about her called Warrior Spirit.
